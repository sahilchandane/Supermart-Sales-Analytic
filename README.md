# Supermart-Sales-Analytic
Designed a comprehensive Super Mart Sales Analytics Dashboard that visualizes business performance, identifies growth opportunities, and supports strategic decision-making through real-time insights.
# Business problem
- To analyze supermarket sales data and identify key factors influencing revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance in order to improve overall business efficiency and growth.
  # KPI's
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales: $1.57M
- Total Profit: $175.26K
- Quantity Sold: 22K
- Analysis Period: 2019 – 2020
- Regions: East, West, Central, South
 ## Dashboard Insights
# Sales Analysis
- Office Supplies generated the highest revenue.
 - Technology and Furniture were strong-performing categories.
 - Phones, Chairs, and Binders emerged as top-selling subcategories.
# Customer Analysis
- Consumer segment contributed the highest sales.
- Corporate customers provided stable revenue.
- Home Office segment showed growth potential.
# Payment Analysis
- Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred payment method.
- Online payments showed significant adoption growth.
# Shipping Analysis
- Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping method.
- customers preferred cost-effective delivery options.
# Regional & Geographic Analysis
- Sales were distributed across multiple U.S. states.
- Certain regions and states contributed significantly higher revenue and profit.
# Trend Analysis
- Sales and profit showed consistent growth from 2019 to 2020.
- Strong business performance was observed during the final quarter of the year.

  # Conclusion
  The Super Mart Sales Analytics Dashboard successfully transformed retail transaction data into meaningful business insights, revealing key trends in sales, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance. The analysis showed $1.57M in total sales, 22K units sold, and $175.26K profit, indicating strong business growth over the analysis period. The Consumer segment emerged as the largest revenue contributor, while Office Supplies and Phones were the top-performing category and sub-category respectively. Additionally, Cash on Delivery and Standard Class shipping were the most preferred customer choices.
   The dashboard also highlighted significant growth in both sales and profit from 2019 to 2020, with peak performance occurring during the final months of the year. Based on these insights, the business should focus on expanding high-performing product categories, strengthening customer retention strategies, promoting digital payment adoption, and investing in high-revenue regions. Overall, this project demonstrates how data-driven analytics can improve decision-making, enhance operational efficiency, and support long-term business growth
